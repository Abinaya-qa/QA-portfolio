# QA Portfolio — Abinaya

## Hi, I am Abinaya 👋

I have been working at Namecheap for 3.5 years as a Customer Technical
Support specialist. Every day I help customers resolve DNS issues, update
host records and nameservers, and register, renew, reactivate and restore
domains.

Over time I noticed something about the way I work. When a customer
comes to me with a problem, I never just fix it and move on. I always
want to know why it happened. Because if I understand why, I can solve
similar cases faster in the future — and sometimes prevent them from
happening at all.

That question — why is this happening — is what led me to QA.
Testing is built around that exact question. Why did this break?
What caused it? How do we make sure it does not happen again?

This portfolio documents my hands-on QA practice as I make that
transition — from someone who fixes problems after they reach customers,
to someone who catches them before they do.

## What I work with every day

In my support role I handle:
- DNS troubleshooting — propagation delays, misconfiguration, record errors
- Host records — A, CNAME, MX, TXT record setup and updates
- Nameserver changes — pointing domains to correct nameservers
- Domain lifecycle — registration, renewal, reactivation, restoration

This daily product knowledge directly shapes how I think about
testing — I already know what breaks, where it breaks, and why.
